Andrew Yen

2D Animator

Hi, my name is Andrew Yen. I am a 2D character and effects animator from Taiwan. I am currently entering my fourth year at the Savannah College of Art and Design for my BFA Animation degree. I enjoy working on stories with horror aspects and eerie atmospheres. I love storytelling as well as creating visuals to convey my stories to a greater audience. My work focuses on character animation, visual effects, and compositing.

In my free time, I enjoy watching movies and anime, as well as all sorts of media from novels to superhero comic books. I also enjoy gaming and writing wild stories based on my imagination.

I have been intrigued by animations since I was little, with Pixar movies and anime. The possibility of bringing my imagination and characters to life with animation struck me. The idea of making only a few panels of my heroes punching villains into a full movie-like action sequence sounded brilliant. It was then that I became determined to learn animation and make my characters move like they are action movie stars.

I hope to contribute to projects that inspire others the way superheroes and animated worlds inspired me—to remind people of the power of imagination and the joy of seeing them come to life.
